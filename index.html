In Memoriam
October 14, 1999 · London, England
···
🕊️
Julius Kambarage Nyerere
April 13, 1922 — October 14, 1999
It is with profound sadness that we share the passing of Julius Kambarage Nyerere, the Mwalimu — "The Teacher" — of Africa. Father of the Tanzanian nation, first President of Tanzania (1964–1985), founder of TANU, and tireless champion of African unity and human dignity. He passed peacefully in London at the age of 77, after a battle with leukemia, surrounded by his family. He led his country to independence without a single shot. He was the conscience of Africa. Rest in peace, Mwalimu. 🌍
#Mwalimu #Tanzania #RIPNyerere #FatherOfTheNation
