Basic information
Full nameJulius Kambarage Nyerere
Date of birthApril 13, 1922 · Butiama, Tanganyika
Date of deathOctober 14, 1999 · London, England (Leukemia, age 77)
GenderMale
Ethnic groupZanaki (Abhakibhweege clan)
Languages spokenSuajili (Kiswahili) · English · Zanaki
About meFirst President of Tanzania (1964–1985) and founding father of the nation. Known as Mwalimu . Founder of TANU (Tanganyika African National Union) and architect of African Socialism — Ujamaa. First African head of state to voluntarily retire from power. Translated Julius Caesar and The Merchant of Venice into Swahili. Described as the "conscience of Africa."
